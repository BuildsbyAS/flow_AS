Direction B Visual bars — plan vs actual

Plan as gray bar, actual as colored bar underneath. The overshoot gets a hatched red tail so the slip is impossible to miss. Best when you want the proportion of slip visible (e.g., a 5-day slip on a 4-day plan is huge).
