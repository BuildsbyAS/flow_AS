Activity Timeline — four directions

Same data, four treatments. Each uses the live sample from the X40 deep-dive: 1 current entry, 2 done + 2 from Mar 16, 3 done + 1 carried from Mar 2, 2 done from Feb 23. Pick the one that matches what you have in mind, or mix pieces.

Direction A True timeline — left rail with dots

A vertical spine anchors the whole section, with colored dots for each entry. Reads top-to-bottom as a sequence of events. Most "timeline-feel." Works best when you want the scroll itself to tell a story.
